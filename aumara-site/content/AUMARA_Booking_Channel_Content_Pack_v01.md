# 2. House Category Copy

## AUMARA Chalet — Short OTA Copy

A private Costa Blanca house for couples, small families and quiet long weekends. AUMARA Chalet gives you the independence of a private stay with the support of the EL CID hospitality ecosystem nearby.

Ideal for slow mornings, terrace coffee, beach days, inland villages, mountain light and dinners connected to EL CID.

## AUMARA Chalet — Long OTA Copy

AUMARA Chalet is designed for travellers who want more privacy than a hotel room, but more warmth and support than a random villa rental.

The house is part of the AUMARA / EL CID hospitality field in Benidoleig, inland Costa Blanca: a quiet base close to beaches, villages, markets, walks, restaurants and day trips.

It is best for couples, small families, friends, quiet weekends and longer stays where the house itself becomes part of the rhythm: morning coffee, outdoor time, simple cooking, slow evenings and access to the wider EL CID food and hospitality layer.

This is a calm stay. No parties, no uncontrolled events, no fake luxury. The value is privacy, space, light, quiet and a hosted environment around the house.

## AUMARA Superior — Short OTA Copy

A larger private Costa Blanca house for families, retreats and slower hosted stays. AUMARA Superior offers more space, privacy and comfort while staying connected to EL CID hospitality, food and local experience.

## AUMARA Superior — Long OTA Copy

AUMARA Superior is the larger house format within the AUMARA / EL CID hospitality ecosystem.

It is made for families, small groups, retreat guests and travellers who need more space than a standard stay. The house gives privacy and independence, while the EL CID layer provides a sense of support: arrival guidance, local orientation, food connection and future retreat / hosted-stay programming.

The house is located in inland Costa Blanca, close enough to the sea for beach days and coastal dinners, but quiet enough to feel removed from the rush.

AUMARA Superior is not positioned as a party villa. It is a calm private-house stay for people who want space, silence, nature, food, family time and a slower rhythm.

# 5. Policy Copy

## Minimum Stay

Minimum stay is normally 2 nights. One-night stays may be accepted only when they fill a calendar gap and can be managed safely by the team.

## Check-in

Check-in instructions are sent before arrival once the booking details are confirmed. Depending on the house and timing, arrival may be self check-in or hosted check-in.

## Check-out

Guests are asked to leave the house in a respectful condition and report any damage or issue before departure.

## Quiet Stay / No Parties

AUMARA is a quiet stay environment. Parties, uncontrolled events, loud music and disruptive behaviour are not permitted.

## Pets

Pets are accepted only by prior approval and may depend on the specific house, cleaning conditions and dates.

## Damage / Deposit

A damage guarantee or deposit may be required depending on the booking channel, length of stay and house type.

## Cleaning

Final cleaning is included or charged according to the booking channel and rate plan. Additional cleaning may be arranged for longer stays.

## Food / Breakfast / Restaurant

Food options, breakfasts, restaurant dinners or retreat menus may be available depending on dates, house occupancy and EL CID kitchen schedule. These should be requested in advance.
